Traveler's Experience

Finally made it to Santorini! The white buildings against the blue sea are even more stunning in person. Spent the day exploring narrow streets and watching the most beautiful sunset I've ever seen from Oia. Definitely worth the hype!

Traveler Tips:

  • Visit Oia for sunset, but arrive early to get a good spot
  • Try the local wine and seafood - especially at Amoudi Bay
  • Rent an ATV to explore the island - much easier than driving
  • Book accommodations with caldera views months in advance
